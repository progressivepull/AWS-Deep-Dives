AWS Simple Email Service (SES) is a scalable email-sending service designed for high-volume email delivery. It is ideal for use cases such as sending up to one million emails or more.

- SES supports **email templates**, making it easy to send consistent and dynamic messages.
- It includes standard **email security features**, such as Sender Policy Framework (SPF), to help ensure email authenticity and deliverability.
- The service is commonly used for application-generated emails, notifications, and marketing communications.

This lesson introduces SES, focusing on its core features and general use cases.
