# 14.1 Learning objectives 
 
- Introduction to **Lesson 13: Simple Email Service (SES)**
- **AWS SES** is an AWS-managed service for sending emails
- Designed for **high-volume email delivery** (e.g., millions of emails)
- Supports **email templates** for reusable and consistent messaging
- Provides standard email security features, including:
  - **Sender Policy Framework (SPF)**
- Lesson objectives:
  - Review key **SES features**
  - Understand general **SES use cases and usage**
